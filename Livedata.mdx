
---
title: "Controll Devices"
description: "This endpoint can update the state of a device, or of any pin of that device."
---

### Body

<ParamField body="command" type="string">
  This variable is used internally by the api. It is recommended to no change it in any way.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="deviceid" type="string">
  This is the ID of the device to be controlled.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="onoff1" type="number 1 or 0">
  This field is used to turn the pin 1 on or off.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="onoff2" type="number 1 or 0">
  This field is used to turn the pin 2 on or off.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="onoff3" type="number 1 or 0">
  This field is used to turn the pin 3 on or off.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="onoff4" type="number 1 or 0">
  This field is used to turn the pin 4 on or off.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="onoff5" type="number 1 or 0">
  This field is used to turn the pin 5 on or off.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="onoff6" type="number 1 or 0">
  This field is used to turn the pin 6 on or off.
</ParamField>
